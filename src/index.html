The state of teacher contract negotiations

Teacher contracts in virtually every school district in Washington state re-opened for negotiation this year. While a handful of districts and their teachers unions struck deals early in the summer to provide raises for educators, teacher walkouts have delayed the first day of school or threaten to do so in about two dozen districts, including Seattle.
